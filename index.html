The New Standard of Distinguished Living: Kolte Patil Goregaon West

Kolte Patil Goregaon West is an upcoming luxury residential project by Kolte Patil Developers, featuring exclusive 2 and 3 BHK apartments designed with thoughtful planning. The project in Bangur Nagar brings together comfort, accessibility, and current design trends. The spacious homes use intelligent design for sufficient airflow and stylish aesthetics. Residents can enjoy an elevated lifestyle with upscale facilities and close proximity to key transport and social infrastructure. About Kolte Patil Developers

Since its founding in 1991, Kolte-Patil Developers, headquartered in Pune, has been guided by the philosophy of 'Creation, not construction.' The company is known for developing high-quality residential and commercial spaces, focusing on innovative designs, timely delivery, and a commitment to excellence. With a strong presence in Pune, Mumbai, and Bengaluru, Kolte-Patil is listed on NSE and BSE, reflecting its credibility and strong market presence in the real estate sector.

Kolte-Patil Developers Ltd. has a legacy of over 3 decades and is one of the most trusted names in the real estate industry, known for its commitment to quality and transparency.
